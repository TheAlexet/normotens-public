# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 19, 2026

Welcome to **Normotens**.

This Privacy Policy explains how Normotens may collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

Normotens may collect the following types of information:

- Account information, such as your email address, if you create an account.

- Device and technical information, such as app version, device type, operating system, and crash data.
- Usage information, such as how you interact with features inside the app.

We may use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the app.

## 3. Health Information

Normotens may allow you to store health-related information for personal tracking purposes.

- Normotens is not a medical service and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
- You should consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following cases:

- With service providers that help us operate the app.
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
- To protect rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or the public.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## 7. Your Choices

Depending on how Normotens works in your version of the app, you may be able to:

- Access or update your information.
- Delete certain records.
- Stop using the app and uninstall it at any time.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Normotens is not intended for children unless explicitly stated otherwise. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where prohibited by law.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we may update the "Last updated" date above.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the app developer through the contact method provided in the app store listing or inside the app.
